Mr.P.Senthil Kumar

Founder & Chairman

Mr. P. Senthil Kumar’s vision guides both CAAS Research Foundation and POLL MATRIX. He believes that data-driven democracy is key to India’s political progress.

Decoding the Pulse of Indian Democracy

We are committed to decoding the pulse of Indian democracy through cutting-edge data analytics, field intelligence, and academic rigor. POLL MATRIX studies how India votes, why it votes, and what those choices mean for governance, policy, and social transformation.

Our Vision

To emerge as India's premier center of excellence in electoral research and analytics, creating a bridge between citizens' voices and policy action through technology-driven, socially inclusive research.

Our Mission

To strengthen Indian democracy by delivering independent, ethical, and innovative research that informs governance, shapes public discourse, and fosters civic awareness.

Our Core Values

Our work is guided by principles that ensure the quality, credibility, and impact of our insights.

Impact

Translating data into meaningful political and policy insights.
