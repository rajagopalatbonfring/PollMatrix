Empowering a Data-Driven Democracy

Building a national knowledge infrastructure for democracy through collaboration, innovation, and education.

Short-Term Courses

Guided by the visionary leadership of Mr. P. Senthil Kumar, both CAAS Research Foundation and POLL MATRIX are committed to transforming India's political landscape through innovation, technology, and education. Mr. Kumar believes that a data-driven democracy is the cornerstone of India's political progress.

Our institutions serve as platforms for cutting-edge research and training in political science, artificial intelligence, data analytics, and technology-led governance. The courses are crafted to blend academic knowledge with real-world application, ensuring participants gain both theoretical and practical expertise.

Course 01

Social Media & Digital Campaigning

Master the art of digital voter engagement, sentiment tracking, and online political branding. Learn how to leverage social media platforms for effective political communication and campaign management.

Course 02

Robotics & Automation in Field Research

Discover how robotic technology and automation tools revolutionize real-time data collection in political research. Explore innovative methods for field surveys and voter data gathering.

Course 03

Electoral Behavior & Predictive Modelling

Learn advanced behavioral science techniques combined with predictive AI tools for accurate election forecasting. Understand voter patterns and develop data-driven campaign strategies.

Course 04

Basic Constitution for Political Leadership

An introduction to the Indian Constitution, focusing on its key principles, structure, fundamental rights, and the role of leaders in upholding democratic values and constitutional governance.

Course 05

Basic Economics for Political Leadership

Covers essential economic concepts such as GDP, inflation, budgeting, and fiscal policy to help emerging political leaders understand and navigate India's economic landscape in policy-making.

Course 06

Basic Socio-Economic Development in India

Explores key issues in India's development including poverty, education, healthcare, and infrastructure, linking socio-economic indicators with political strategies and grassroots-level governance.

Course 07

Stratification of Indian Society and Politics

Analyzes caste, class, religion, and gender dynamics in India, highlighting their influence on electoral politics, policy decisions, and representation in democratic institutions.

Course 08

Nationalism and Politics

Traces the evolution of nationalism in India and its role in shaping political ideologies, movements, party systems, and national identity in the post-independence political framework.
