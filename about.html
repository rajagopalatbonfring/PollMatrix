About POLL MATRIX

Born from the pioneering work of the Centre for Advanced Analysis and Strategies (CAAS Research Foundation).

Our History

For nearly two decades, CAAS Research Foundation has been at the forefront of policy analysis and socio-economic studies. Building on this legacy, POLL MATRIX was conceptualized as a dedicated electoral analytics and democratic research division.

Evolution to Specialization

Recognizing the growing need for scientific, unbiased electoral research, CAAS evolved POLL MATRIX to examine voter psychology, electoral reforms, governance impact, and digital influence. We merge academic integrity with applied political science.

What We Study

  • Voter psychology and behavior.
  • Electoral reforms and democratic participation.
  • Governance, policy impact, and leadership evaluation.
  • Digital influence, misinformation, and media narratives.

Chairman's Vision

Chairman Image

“Indian democracy is not merely a political system—it is a living conversation between the people and those who govern them. Research is the voice of that conversation, and data is its truth.”

— Mr. P. Senthil Kumar, Founder & Chairman

The vision of Mr. P. Senthil Kumar drives both CAAS Research Foundation and POLL MATRIX. His belief is simple yet transformative: that data-driven democracy is the future of India’s political progress. Under his stewardship, POLL MATRIX was founded as a democratic intelligence network—a platform that seeks to transform raw public sentiment into structured knowledge, and knowledge into strategic governance.

Chairman's Message

Mr. Senthil Kumar envisions POLL MATRIX as a bridge—a conduit that connects the lived realities of citizens with the decision-making corridors of power. He believes that effective governance cannot operate in isolation from citizen emotion and expectation. This process bridges not just information gaps but also trust deficits.
For Mr. Senthil Kumar, integrity in research is the moral backbone of democracy. In an age where misinformation and political sensationalism can distort public discourse, POLL MATRIX has committed itself to transparency, verification, and scientific authenticity. He advocates for polling and data analytics that serve public knowledge rather than partisan interest.
Beyond polling and prediction, the Chairman’s vision extends toward building a national knowledge infrastructure for democracy—a collaborative ecosystem that unites researchers, journalists, policymakers, and citizens. He envisions POLL MATRIX as a democratic think hub, where diverse disciplines converge to produce research that shapes both electoral outcomes and governance reform.
Mr. Senthil Kumar often refers to research as “the conscience of democracy.” For him, research is not a mechanical process of counting opinions—it is an act of national introspection. It reflects who we are as a people, what we expect from leadership, and how we measure progress.
